Incredibly Hygienic

After every ride, our drivers are quick to sanitize all of the equipment with pet-friendly sanitizing liquid to ensure every pet gets the same hygienic experience. Not only that, we also coat our equipment with high quality, safe and pet-safe tick powder. This ensures that we are able to uphold Jojo’s incredibly strict hygienic requirements.

Certified Drivers

Drivers at Jojo Pets go through a strict background check in order to ensure that they are capable in making sure your pet arrives at the destination safe and happy.

High Quality Safety Equipment

Every pet bed is equipped with high quality safety harnesses in order to keep your pet safe in the event of any accidents. From reinforced kevlar straps, to soft safety padding; your pet will arrive at the destination safe and sound.

Video Recording Feature

After every ride, Jojo Pets will provide users with a video detailing their pet’s journey after every ride. This is for our users to know that Jojo Pets is serious about the safety and well-being of your pets. With all of these features, we are definitely the safest pet transportation service in Malaysia!
